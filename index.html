Biography

Jingdong Wang is a Senior Researcher at Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia. His areas of interest include efficient CNN architecture design, human pose estimation, semantic segmentation, image classification, object detection, large-scale indexing, person re-identification, and salient object detection. He has been serving/served as an Associate Editor of IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TMM and IEEE TCSVT, and an area chair (or SPC) of some prestigious conferences in vision, multimedia, and AI, such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ACM MM, IJCAI, and AAAI. He is an ACM Distinguished Member and a Fellow of IAPR. CV
