Biography

Jingdong Wang is a Senior Principal Research Manager with Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia. His areas of interest include neural architecture design, human pose estimation, semantic segmentation, image classification, object detection, large-scale indexing, and salient object detection. He has been serving/served as an Associate Editor of IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TMM and IEEE TCSVT, and an area chair (or SPC) of some prestigious conferences in vision, multimedia, and AI, such as CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ACM MM, IJCAI, and AAAI. He is an ACM Distinguished Member and a Fellow of IAPR. CV
